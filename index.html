About the Foundation

Serving Nembe Since 2002

Founded by Korite Michael Kalango, the Kalango Helping People's Foundation has dedicated over two decades to supporting underprivileged community members in the Niger Delta. We believe that true growth begins when we lift others up.

Our End of Year Drive

Empowering the Needful This December

We have designed targeted programs to address immediate needs and spread joy. Here is how your donations will make a direct difference in Nembe.

Mobility

Wheelchairs & Accessibility

Many disabled individuals in our community are housebound. We are purchasing and distributing high-quality, durable wheelchairs to give them independence, freedom of movement, and restored dignity.

Target: 50 Wheelchairs 75,000 NGN per unit
Nutrition

Festive Food & Essentials

No family should go hungry during the festive season. We distribute food packs containing rice, cooking oil, beans, and season items to widows, orphans, and elders in Nembe.

Target: 300 Families 5,000 NGN per pack
Joy

New Year & Christmas Party

We host community-wide parties for children and elders. Complete with festive meals, music, gifts, and games, we bring laughter and holiday spirit directly to the heart of the community.

Target: 500 Children 3,000 NGN per child
📚 Education Support
Education

Student Scholarships & Supplies

Empowering the next generation. We supply underprivileged school children with bags, books, uniforms, and sponsor term fees to ensure they continue their education in the upcoming school year.

Target: 150 Students 20,000 NGN per term
💼 Livelihoods
Empowerment

Empowerment Grants

Helping widows and youths build financial resilience. We offer micro-grants and trade tools (like sewing machines, hairdressing tools, or seed stock) to help them start self-sustaining retail businesses.

Target: 30 Traders 50,000 NGN grant
